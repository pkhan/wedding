Getting to Menlo Park/Palo Alto

The three major airports are San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose. San Francisco is the closest and most convenient for those using public transportation.

You can take public transit, rent a car or use a taxi service to get around. All of the wedding venues are reachable via public transit and a short (less than 10 minute) walk.

The directions below will get you to the three block-rate hotels. If you are going directly to the Church of the Nativity, you will need to go to the Menlo Park CalTrain station.

Public transit from the San Francisco Airport (SFO)

  1. Follow the signs to BART
  2. Take the BART one stop South to Millbrae (they charge $4.25 for this one stop, unfortunately. Still cheaper than a taxi). During the daytime on weekdays, you must go north one stop to San Bruno and then south one stop to Millbrae
  3. Next you'll need to transfer to the CalTrain. Make sure you buy a CalTrain ticket before you get on the CalTrain. This should cost $5.25.
  4. Take the CalTrain South (direction: San Jose) and get off at the Palo Alto stop

Public transit from San Jose (SJC)

  1. Take the number 10 shuttle towards the Santa Clara CalTrain Station.
  2. Take the CalTrain towards San Francisco.
  3. Get off at the Palo Alto stop (cost will be $5.25 -- be sure to purchase a ticket before getting on the train).

Public Transit from Oakland (OAK)

  1. Take the BART to Milbrae, then follow the directions from SFO from step 3

Getting Around

We strongly recommend using Uber or Lyft if you have access to a smart phone and are not renting a car. This will be the easiest way to get from the ceremony to the reception. There are numerous Uber and Lyft drivers in the area so you shouldn't have to wait much time at all.

If you are planning to travel to San Francisco while staying in the Palo Alto area, the CalTrain is your easiest option. The CalTrain can get you up to AT&T park along the bay. From there, you can request an Uber or transfer to the SF Muni bus and light rail system.

Parking

Parking a the Church of the Nativity

Ample parking is available in the lot of the church.

Parking at the Garden Court Hotel

Complimentary valet parking is available at the Garden Court until it reaches capacity.

Public parking is available at no cost in city lots at Cowper/Hamilton, next to the Garden Court Hotel and the Webster/Cowper parking garage with an entrance across from the Garden Court Hotel.
